Preliminary results of comparing rule based TGen and equational TGen
in 3 CP2 scenarios.   There is a big difference in 
rewrites and time for DNS, very little for Mastodon.
The reason appears to be that the rate of sending 
for the DNS user models is 10-20 times that of Mastodon,
making the Mastodon TGen have very little effect.

NGT -- number of traffic generator actors
NFi -- the number of files transmitted
<...>O  --- original specification using rules 
<...>E  --- with rules replaced by equations
1131 sec = 19min
